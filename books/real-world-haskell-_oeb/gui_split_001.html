Chapter 23. GUI Programming with gtk2hs

Throughout this book, we have been developing simple text-based tools. While these are often ideal interfaces, sometimes a graphical user interface (GUI) is required. There are several GUI toolkits available for Haskell. In this chapter, we will look at one of them, gtk2hs.[55]

[55] Several alternatives also exist. Alongside gtk2hs, wxHaskell is also a prominent cross-platform GUI toolkit.
